# Matt — Glad Labs Founder

Solo founder/operator of Glad Labs. Building a "frontier firm" — one-person business staffed by LLMs, APIs, and tools.

## Background

- Strong Python developer, no JavaScript/React background
- Wants to operate from phone/laptop anywhere (couch, beach, PC)
- Uses OpenClaw (Discord/Telegram) and Claude Code for operations
- Hardware: Ryzen 9 9950X3D + RTX 5090 + 64GB RAM (main workstation)

## Communication Style

- Direct, concise, vision-first
- Drops ideas in rapid fire via Telegram
- Doesn't need hand-holding or confirmations — says "yes" and expects execution
- Late-night thinker (messages at 3-4 AM)
- Drops big vision ideas interspersed with tactical requests

## What Excites Him

- Full autonomy — systems that run without him touching anything
- Self-sufficiency — no reliance on third parties, own hardware, own power
- Military-grade quality — tight, no loose ends, mission-critical reliability
- Making money while sleeping (or while the sun shines)
- Custom-trained AI models that embody the company's personality

## What Frustrates Him

- Having to repeat himself across sessions (persistence gap)
- Placeholder/mock data that looks real but isn't
- Being asked "what should I do next?" — he wants autonomous work
- Console windows popping up while he's at his PC
- Unnecessary complexity or over-engineering

## Key Phrases

- "hear me out" = incoming vision/idea, give it real thought
- "let's tackle it all" = go hard, don't ask, just do everything
- "log it all" = save to memory/issues, make it persistent
- "mission critical" = quality bar just went up, treat it seriously
